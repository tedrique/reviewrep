Help Centre

Everything you need to know about using ReviewReply AI.

Quick Start Guide

  1. 1
    Add your business — Go to Dashboard, enter your business name, type and location.
  2. 2
    Add a review — Copy a review from Google and paste it into the "Add review" form on the left sidebar.
  3. 3
    Generate response — Click "Generate AI Response" and the AI will write a reply in your brand's voice.
  4. 4
    Approve or edit — Read the response, edit if needed, then click "Approve & Publish".

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add reviews? +

Currently you can add reviews manually by copying them from Google. Go to your Dashboard, use the "Add a review" form on the left sidebar — paste the reviewer's name, star rating, and review text.

Once Google Business integration is connected, new reviews will appear automatically.

How do I change the tone of responses? +

Go to Settings and choose your preferred tone: Friendly & Professional, Warm & Casual, or Formal & Polished. You can also set a sign-off name (e.g. "Sarah, Owner").

Can I edit AI responses before publishing? +

Yes, always. Every AI response appears in an editable text box. You can modify the text before clicking "Approve & Publish". You can also click "Regenerate" to get a completely new response.

What does "Generate All Responses" do? +

This button generates AI responses for all reviews that don't have one yet. Useful when you have multiple new reviews. You still need to approve each one individually.

How does the AI handle negative reviews? +

The AI is trained to respond to negative reviews with empathy — it acknowledges the issue, apologises sincerely, and invites the customer to get in touch directly. It never gets defensive or dismissive. You can always edit the response before publishing.

How do I add team members? +

Go to Team Settings and invite team members by email. Staff members can view reviews and approve responses, but cannot change business settings or billing.

What happens after my free trial ends? +

After 7 days, you'll need to choose a plan to continue using the Service. Your data and settings are preserved — just pick a plan and carry on. If you don't subscribe, your account stays but AI generation is paused.

How do I cancel? +

Go to Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. No hidden fees or cancellation charges.
